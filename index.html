About Us

Sola is a game developed by students of Reddam House middle school. We are attempting to create a game which provides unique and fun enjoyment, that is playable by a wide audience.

The Game

Sola is a real time stratergy game based in the far distant future in a solar system far far away... you will be playing as an empire making your first steps into the cosmos.

Development

Sola was developed in Unity using tools such as Blender, and Photoshop to accomplish most of the design. We developed this in 5 weeks, and are proud of our accomplishments.
